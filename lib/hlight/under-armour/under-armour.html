Under Armour® was founded in Baltimore, Maryland in 1996 and has since become a leading developer, marketer and distributor of branded performance apparel, footwear and accessories.

Notable product lines include HeatGear and ColdGear apparel and the Speedform footwear range. The brand's products are sold worldwide and worn by athletes at all levels, from youth to professional, on playing fields and gyms across the globe.

For Under Armour, the mission has always been to make all athletes better through passion, design and the relentless pursuit of innovation.
