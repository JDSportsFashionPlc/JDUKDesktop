Lacoste

Originating back to 1933 when founded René Lacoste replaced the classic woven fabric, long-sleeved and starched shirts on the tennis courts with the now iconic polo shirt, the Lacoste brand has become renowned around the world for their quality clothing and footwear. Now a firm favourite with lovers of style as opposed to the stars of tennis, Lacoste still prides its success on authenticity, performance and elegance. Now offering everything from t-shirts and tracksuits to shorts and hoodies, Lacoste have also developed a reputation for their stylishly simple trainers such as the instantly Lacoste Marcel. A must-have addition to any wardrobe, the Lacoste brand looks set to stand the test of time.
