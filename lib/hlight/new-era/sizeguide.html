How to get a cap to fit your noggin!

How to measure your head:

The average men's head size is 7 3/8 (US Sizing) or 58 cm (European Sizing) or 71/8 (UK Sizing), however to make sure you find the right size for you it is important to measure your own head before you purchase.

Simply take a measuring tape and place it around the circumference of the head, slightly above the ears. As a guide here are the standard hat sizes on offer.
